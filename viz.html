Where memes from the past decade came from

Know Your Meme, a website that tracks the history and origins of memes, analyzed a decade of meme data, to determine from which platforms did the most memes originate.

It's interesting to see how at the beginning of the decade Youtube and 4chan generated 60%-70% of memes, but now Twitter and TikTok seemed to have replaced these platforms. 4chan has experienced a steep decline, with their meme origin percentage at around 1%.

While the chart is interesting to look at, there is a lack of contrast due to the usage of different shades of blue both in the background and in the lines used to represent the platforms. Changing the color of the background will help increase the contrast and improve readability.
